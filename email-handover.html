Automated Email Handover System

← Back to Portfolio

To address the problems of manual email handovers between shifts, We developed an Automated Email Handover System using Python. The system automatically collects daily task information and creates standardized handover reports, which are then sent to team members across 4 International sites at scheduled times. By eliminating manual data entry and formatting, the automation increased reporting accuracy by 70% and ensured consistent information sharing between shifts. This simple but effective solution has significantly reduced miscommunication and improved workflow continuity across all shift transitions, making team handovers more reliable and efficient.
