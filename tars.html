TARS – Ticket Assignee & Removal System

Situation

High ticket volume was slowing down resolution across global teams, requiring manual triaging.

Action

Built a Python-based system (TARS) that auto-assigned and cleaned up tickets across queues in real time.

Result

Reduced manual workload by 78%, improved turnaround time, and enabled 290+ daily ticket operations to run smoothly.
