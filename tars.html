TARS – Ticket Assignee & Removal System

← Back to Portfolio

To address delays caused by high ticket volumes across global sites, We developed TARS (Ticket Assignee & Removal System), a Python-based solution for automated ticket management. The system handles ticket assignments in real-time and automatically cleans up resolved tickets, eliminating the need for manual intervention. This automation resulted in a 78% reduction in administrative workload, allowing support teams to focus on actual problem-solving. TARS now efficiently manages over 290+ daily tasks, significantly improving ticket resolution times and overall service delivery across 4 International Sites. The system's implementation has streamlined the entire ticketing process, making it more efficient and responsive to user needs.
